Top Attractions

Tikal National Park

Tikal is one of the largest archaeological sites of the ancient Maya civilization. It is located in the rainforests of northern Guatemala and is famous for its towering pyramids, temples, and palaces.

Antigua Guatemala

Antigua is a charming colonial city known for its well-preserved Spanish Baroque-influenced architecture, stunning churches, and vibrant cultural festivals.

Lake Atitlán

Lake Atitlán is a beautiful volcanic lake surrounded by picturesque villages, lush mountains, and three towering volcanoes. It is a popular destination for outdoor activities and cultural experiences.
