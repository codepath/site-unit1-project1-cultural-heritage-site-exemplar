Food Guide

Gusina Garífuna

Address: 4 Avenida 3-56, Cdad. de Guatemala 01001, Guatemala

Experience the unique flavors of the Caribbean and Garifuna cuisine, including shrimp ceviche, grilled sea snail, and traditional dishes like tapado and machuca. Great for foodies and seafood lovers.

Doña Elsa

Address: 8 Calle, Cdad. de Guatemala, Guatemala

Discover the comfort of home-cooked Guatemalan dishes at Doña Elsa's stall in Mercado Central, featuring caldo de gallina, carne guisada, and hilachas. Ideal for families and travelers seeking authentic local cuisine.

Visit Website

San Martín Centro Histórico

Address: Centro Histórico, 6A Avenida 10-00, Cdad. de Guatemala 01001, Guatemala

Enjoy a variety of Guatemalan egg dishes and international breakfast plates in a charming 18th-century Colonial home, complemented by a cup of Guatemalan coffee. Perfect for coffee lovers and those interested in historical settings.
