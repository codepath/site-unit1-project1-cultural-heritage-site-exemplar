Explore the Heart of the Mayan World

Embark on a journey through Travel Guatemala, your ultimate guide to exploring the rich tapestry of culture, history, and natural beauty that defines this enchanting land. Uncover the secrets of the ancient Maya at the majestic ruins of Tikal, immerse yourself in the vibrant local culture, and find tranquility by the crystal-clear waters of Lake Atitlán. Your adventure awaits—dive into the heart of Guatemala and discover its wonders for yourself!

Quick Facts About Guatemala

  • Location: Central America
  • Capital: Guatemala City
  • Language: Spanish and 21 Mayan languages
  • Cultural Heritage: Textiles, folk dances, cuisine
  • Landscapes: Volcanoes, rainforests, Lake Atitlán
